About the MiCAR Tracker

A clearer view of MiCAR activity in Europe

The MiCAR Tracker is created by the Digital Euro Association (DEA) to make the European crypto-asset landscape easier to understand. We collect Electronic Money Token (EMT) issuer and Crypto-Asset Service Provider (CASP) registrations from the European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) and present them in a friendly, interactive dashboard.

Built on open data and open collaboration

Transparency is central to our mission. The tracker is fully open source, allowing the community to inspect and improve how we process regulatory information. We source data directly from ESMA‚Äôs interim MiCAR register, clean it in collaboratively maintained Google Spreadsheets, and transform it into a beautiful, readable overview on this website.

The Digital Euro Association empowers its members by combining education, community building, and public affairs advocacy. The MiCAR Tracker supports that mission by helping policymakers, industry leaders, and researchers stay informed about regulatory progress and market participation across Europe.
